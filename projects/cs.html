    • Candidate Site

    Timeline: September 2022 - December 2022

    Role: Lead Developer

    Collaborators: Michigan EcoData

    TL;DR: Developed a website to inform Michigan voters of their political candidates’ stances on environmental policies leading up to the 2022 midterm election.

    Skills: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, D3.js

    Check it out: https://cmoy11.github.io/ecodataCandidateSite/

    About

    Michign EcoData is a project based student organization at the University of Michigan that fosters a space for creative, impact driven, and student led projects that address environmental and sustainability issues through data science and technological solutions. In the semeseter leading up to the 2022 Michigan Midterm elections, our project team wanted to develop a hub for Michigan voters to educate themselves on political candidates' stance on differenct environmental policies. Following the election, we populated the site with the winning candidate and data around constituents' beliefs in the same environmental policies. Although now an artifact, the candidate tracking website can still be useful in understanding environmental policy beliefs around Michigan and how that might influence future elections.

    Problem

    In an era of misinformation and mass media, our project team wanted to create a hub for Michigan voters that demystified environmental policies on the ballot.

    Timeline

    1. Candidate Research (4 weeks)
    2. Figma Mockup (1 week)
    3. Website development (2 weeks)
    4. Website release and dissemination (2 Weeks)
    5. Post election website updates and reporting (3 Weeks)

    Challenges

    • Website Accessibility - D3 (the map feature) was challenging to work with and created challenges with website accessibility. We ultimately decided to prioritize accessibility (tabbing though links) to ensure equitibale use of our site.
    • Candiate Research - Unsurprisingly, many candidates were reluctant to publically post views on climate change and environmental policies.

    Outcome

    Overall, our project was quite successful. We were able to complete our research and publish the candidate tracking website well ahead of the midterm elections. Our team primarilly utilized the Michigan EcoData network to publicize and share the resource. In all, we achieved our goal of reaching 250 viewers and were able to archive the website on the Michigan EcoData Website.
