    • About Me

    Hi! I'm Cameron Moy! I am a researcher, data scientist, web developer, and activist. My work addresses disparate impacts of digital platforms and technology on communities of color across disciplines through mixed method approaches.

    Recent projects include...

    [1] Survey of 2332 Detroit residents polling their perceptions of the City of Detroit's Project Green Light, crime, and safety (to appear at FaaCT 2024). [Learn More]

    [2] Quantitative analysis of Yelp's Black-owned attribute's effect on Black-owned restaurants' online visibility and reputation (to appear at ASIS&T 2023, ICA 2024). [Learn More]

    [3] Photovoice study using collaborative photo taking to understand Detroit residents' feelings on community, safety, and surveillance (CHI 2023, DIS 2023). [Learn More]

    [4] Interviews with police officers and community activists in response to growing digital presence of Defund the Police and ACAB movements. [Learn More]
